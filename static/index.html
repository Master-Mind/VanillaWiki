Welcome to VanillaWiki!

For all your vanilla information needs or whatever!

Vanilla is a flavoring derived from orchids of the genus Vanilla, primarily from the Mexican vanilla, flat-leaved vanilla (V. planifolia), derived from V. planifolia, is also grown commercially at the Indian Ocean islands of Madagascar and Reunion and in Indonesia. Vanilla is the second-most expensive spice after saffron because growing the vanilla seed pods is labor-intensive. Despite the expense, vanilla is highly valued for its flavor.

As a result, vanilla is widely used in both commercial and domestic baking, perfume manufacture, and aromatherapy.
